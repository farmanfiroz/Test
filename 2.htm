YJ Chan, Beginner in PhoneGap
Written 4 Sep 2015
For my case,I used phonegap desktop app to create phonegap project and host it in my laptop as a server. You have to install phonegap developer on your mobile device (download from app store and play store) in order to get full function work.
After you have both application in your PC and mobile device, you can run your project in your mobile device.
First, open phonegap desktop then create new project or open existing project. After you have done this step, you will see your project was created and there is two button together with your project, (seem like play and stop)  click the play button. (only when you have clicked the play button, your app can work in phonegap developer)
Then check on your setting, you will see server port, eg. 192.168.1.11:3000 (This is the address that you should using to connect your device with the local server - your pc). Then now go to your mobile device, open phonegap developer, and you will see server address. Make sure the server address is same as what shown on your phonegap desktop app server port address.
(*IMPORTANT, I stucked here for hours, LOL) *
It need you to connect in the same network, if you are connecting wifi, you need both of your device connect to the same wifi.
Then, in phonegap developer, press connect. Finally, you will see your project appears in phonegap developer as a funcational mobile app, just as what you've developer
